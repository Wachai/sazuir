Kiira Cottage - Kedong Ranch, Naivasha

$370/night

Kiira Cottage is set in the pristine grasslands of Kedong ranch only 2.5 Km off Moi South lake road, This is the idea vacation rental for a family or group seeking to enjoy tranquility, the spectacular views of Lake Naivasha, Mt. Longonot and the breathtaking sunsets while savoring sundowners by the pool The cottage boasts of a full span glass wall overlooking the scenic Lake Naivasha. The cottage has an external self contained tented camp which can accommodate two people. Thus bringing the total capacity of the cottage to ten people
